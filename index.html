    Web Technology Projects


    HTML Project: Dreaming Places



    Project 1 consists of creating an informative website.
    The information presented on the website pertains to a place (country, city, or state) in which we wanted to inform others about.

    The place I chose as the topic for my website was Jamaica. I chose Jamaica because I find it to be one of the most beautiful countries in the world and not to mention it is also one of the most popular vacation destinations.

    Visit My Jamaica Project

    HTML and CSS Project: Sales Website



    Project 2 consists of creating a sales webpage. The purpose of Project 2 is to apply the knowledge of colors, image manipulation, animation, slideshow, and modal window using CSS to create a working sales webpage.

    I created a webpage for Bath & Body Works Collection. On this webpage I presented some of Bath & Body Works featured holiday body care products. In the introduction, I created a slideshow to advertise product sales and used hero images to present the body care products. In the product list I presented three lotions and three gift sets of their collection and created a modal window to present each product.

    Visit My Bath & Body Works Collection Project
